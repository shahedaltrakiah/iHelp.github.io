About us

Welcome to our platform! At our core, we're dedicated to enhancing the academic journey for students with special needs at the University of Jordan. Through our software platform, we offer vital support that ensures every university student can access the assistance they require while also encouraging others to volunteer and contribute to this meaningful cause.

Our user-friendly website serves as a hub where potential volunteers can explore guidelines and seamlessly apply for various positions. We understand the diverse needs of students with disabilities, especially when it comes to exams, research, and assignments. That's why our platform provides two distinct interfaces—one for volunteers and another tailored for students with special needs—making it easy for volunteers to access necessary services and apply while considering their availability.

Central to our mission is the streamlining of support services for students with disabilities. Our website functions as a centralized system, efficiently connecting volunteers with those who need their assistance. We maintain a comprehensive database of essential resources, including books and records, for quick access in case of recurring requests.

Ultimately, our project strives to foster a more inclusive and supportive academic environment within the University of Jordan. Join us in creating a community where everyone has equal access to educational resources and support, making a tangible difference in each student's academic journey.
