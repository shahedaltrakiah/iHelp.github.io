FAQ

What is this project about?

Our initiative focuses on providing essential support to students with special needs at the University of Jordan. We aim to create an inclusive environment by facilitating access to resources and encouraging volunteerism.

How can I get involved as a volunteer?

Interested individuals can explore guidelines and apply for volunteer positions through our website. The process is designed to consider factors such as time availability, ensuring a convenient volunteering experience.

What support is provided to students with special needs?

Students with special needs receive streamlined assistance for exams, research, and assignments through our platform. The system coordinates services efficiently, connecting volunteers to those requiring support.

How is information maintained within the platform ?

The platform operates as a centralized system, maintaining a comprehensive database of relevant materials. This ensures easy access to books and records for recurring requests.

How does this initiative enhance the academic environment?

You can help by sharing information about our platform among your peers, encouraging both volunteers and students with special needs to explore the opportunities available.

How can I spread the word about this initiative?

By fostering inclusivity and support for students with disabilities, our project aims to create a more inclusive and supportive academic atmosphere within the University of Jordan.
